Our Vision

We built a BNB node on Solana following Anatoly Yakovenko's original vision for high-performance, decentralized infrastructure. Our implementation focuses on speed, reliability, and seamless integration with the Solana ecosystem.

By adhering to the core principles of Solana's architecture—proof of history, parallel transaction processing, and Gulf Stream forwarding—we've created a node that delivers the performance and decentralization that Toly envisioned from day one.
