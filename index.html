What is issuance?

Cz wanted someone to make a coin where 90% of supply was locked and slowly used to buy back and we created that today

How It Starts

At launch: Only 10% of tokens are sold to the public.

The money raised goes to building the product, marketing, and paying the team.

The other 10% of tokens are locked up.

Three Rules for Unlocking More Tokens

To unlock more tokens, ALL three conditions must be met:

Rule 1: Wait 6 Months

At least 6 months must pass since the last unlock.

Rule 2: Price Must Double

The token price must be 2x higher than the last unlock price, and stay there for 30 days straight.

Rule 3: Maximum 5%

Only up to 5% more tokens can be unlocked each time.

Simple Example

January: Launch at $1 → 10% tokens sold
June: Price is $1.50 → Can't unlock (needs to be $2+)
July 4 to Aug 3: Price stays above $2 for 30 days
August 3: Price is $3 → Can unlock 5% more tokens ✓
Next unlock: Earliest is March (6 months later), and only if price stays above $6 for 30 days

Why This Works

  • No token dumps when price is weak
  • Team is rewarded only when token does well
  • Encourages long-term building, not quick profits
  • Protects token holders from inflation at bad times

Team Control

The team CAN:

  • Wait longer than 6 months
  • Unlock less than 5%
  • Choose not to unlock at all

The team CANNOT:

  • Unlock sooner than 6 months
  • Unlock more than 5% at once
  • Bypass the price requirement
Security: All tokens are locked in a smart contract. A third party holds the keys, so the team can't cheat the rules.
