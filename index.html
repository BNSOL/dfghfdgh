What is bub?

Binance posted a video on LinkedIn showing their new Web3 wallet, and in the video they were holding a memecoin named "bub". However, bub doesn't exist on the BNB chain, so we're remaking it and bringing it to life.

The Story Behind bub

When Binance showcased their new Web3 wallet on LinkedIn, eagle-eyed viewers noticed they were holding a token called "bub" in the demonstration. The community immediately searched for this token on the BNB chain, but it didn't exist.

This sparked the idea: if Binance featured it in their official wallet demo, why not bring bub to life on the BNB chain? That's exactly what we're doing.

Why bub?

  • Featured in Binance's official Web3 wallet video
  • Community-driven creation inspired by Binance's demo
  • A true memecoin born from an authentic moment
  • Bringing what was shown in the video to reality on BNB chain
